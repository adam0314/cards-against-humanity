Cards Against Humanity

The idea is that you play this game exactly the way you would play the cards against humanity card game: You play in person against your friends (with phones instead of cards), while drinking.

Rules

How do you play?

The judge reads the black card aloud, and everyone else picks their best white card to go along with it. The judge reads all of the white cards aloud and chooses one.
